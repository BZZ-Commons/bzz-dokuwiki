====== LU01a - Introduction to DevOps ======
<sup>Internal reference: learningunits/01-1.md</sup>
\\
===== Goals =====
  -You can explain in your own words the **purpose of DevOps** and how it differs from traditional development and operations silos.
  -You can describe **why culture and collaboration** are critical for implementing DevOps successfully.
  -You can list at least **three benefits** that come with DevOps practices.
  -You can list at least **one organizational challenge** that come with DevOps practices.
  -You can provide **examples of how CI/CD pipelines** might look in real projects, including tools like Git, Jenkins, or Docker.

\\
===== Basics =====
In [[en:modul:m324_aws:topics:01|this article]], you will learn about what DevOps is, how they are defined, and their benefits.
