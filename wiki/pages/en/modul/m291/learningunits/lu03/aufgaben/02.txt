====== LU03.A02 - W3School Working Space Allocation ======

===== Learning Objectives =====
  - Know how to enroll a W3School account
  - Allocate working space to save assignment results
  
===== Prequisites  =====
  * Work type: Individual
  * Means of aid: 
    * [[https://www.w3schools.com/vue/index.php | W3School VUE Introduction]]
    * [[https://www.w3schools.com/vue/tryit.php?filename=tryvue_helloWorld|W3School VUE Playground]]
  * Timeframe: 10 Minutes
  * Expected result: Have a working space at your disposal to save assignment results.

===== Introduction  =====
During this module 291, you will work on several assignments in the W3Schools Playground. To save your results, you will need a workspace within the webpage.Fortunately, W3Schools provides us with such a workspace. To use it, you must first create an account and then set up a suitable workspace. Thus, this assignment supports you step by step in setting up your workspace.

===== Assignments =====

The assignment consistes three major steps:
  - Create an W3School-Account
  - Allocate the name and the working space
  - Save working files in your working space and execute them
