====== LU01a - Modul administration ======

===== Learning objectives =====
  - I can explain the Modul 291's learning objectives, which are stated in the module definition.

===== Introduction =====
The learning unit LU01a deals with the management of the contents of the m291 and consists of the following three sub-chapters: 

  - the goals we are supposed to achieve at the end of the modul 291
  - details about the exams and the weighting of such
  - required software, how to install them, and how to check their operational functionality
