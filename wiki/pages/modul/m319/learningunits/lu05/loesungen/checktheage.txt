====== LU05.L05: Checking the age ======

<code python>
def main():
    age = int(input('How old are you?\n'))
    if age >= 0 and age <= 120:
        print('OK')
    else:
        print('Impossible!')


if __name__ == '__main__':
    main()

</code>
