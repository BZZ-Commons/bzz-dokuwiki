====== LU05.L04: Odd or Even ======

<code python>
def main():
    number = int(input('Give a number:\n'))
    if number % 2 == 0:
        print(f'Number {number} is even.')
    else:
        print(f'Number {number} is odd.')


if __name__ == '__main__':
    main()
</code>
