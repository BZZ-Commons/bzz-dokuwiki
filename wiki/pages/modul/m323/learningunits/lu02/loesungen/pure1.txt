====== LU02.L01 - Summe einer Zahlenliste ======

<code python>
def sum_of_numbers(numbers):
    return sum(numbers)

if __name__ == '__main__':
    numbers = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
    print('The sum of the numbers is:', sum_of_numbers(numbers))
</code>
